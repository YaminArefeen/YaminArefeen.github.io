I am working towards my PhD in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at MIT advised by Elfar Adalsteinsson and Berkin Bilgic graduating in May 2023. I recieved a S.M. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from MIT in 2019 and a B.S. in Electrical Engineering from Rice University in 2017.

I am interested in the application of signal processing, optimization, and machine learning to address acquisition and reconstruction problems in Magnetic Resonance Imaging. I am grateful to be funded in part by the National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship and the Neuroimaging Training Program Fellowship.

I am currently searching for academic and industry positions.
